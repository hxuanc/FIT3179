UNICORN STARTUPS

A privately help startup company valued at over US$1 billion are known as a unicorn. The term unicorn was first published in 2013, which is coined by of a mythical animal “unicorn” to represent the rareness of such success. In order to be recognize as an unicorn, companies be innovative, creative and have a clear vision for the future growth.
